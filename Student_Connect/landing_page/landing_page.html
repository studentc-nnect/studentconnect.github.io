After that you can request to join a group to hangout with people of same interests as you.




Profile and Groups.

You have to create a profile with mentioning details about your hobbies and about yourself that represents yourself so that other people can view your profile. Groups are created so that people with same interests can come together to hangout. Join a group with your interests to participate in group activities that you might like doing.
